# Example: 30-Minute Session Cycle

## Parent Request

```text
I have 30 minutes to tutor my child. Use the attached memory files. Recommend one objective, coach me through it, and help me record evidence afterward.
```

## AI Reads

- `00-profile.md`
- `04-goals.md`
- `01-learning-map.md`
- `03-review-queue.md`
- recent entries from `02-evidence-log.md`

## AI Plans

| Time | Parent Action | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 min | Warm up with a previously learned item. | Retention and confidence. |
| 10 min | Teach or revisit one small concept. | Reasoning and misconception evidence. |
| 10 min | Child completes a controlled task. | Assistance level and accuracy. |
| 5 min | Parent asks the child to explain. | Transfer and language evidence. |

## Parent Reports

```text
She solved 3 of 4 independently. She said 2/4 and 1/2 are the same because both shade half the rectangle. She needed a prompt for 3/6 and counted shaded pieces but forgot to compare total equal parts.
```

## AI Produces

- evidence entry for `02-evidence-log.md`
- learning-map update for the relevant skill
- review due date for `03-review-queue.md`
- summary for `05-session-reports.md`
- next recommendation
